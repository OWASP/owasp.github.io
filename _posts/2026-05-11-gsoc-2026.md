## Highlights from the 2026 Project Slate

This year's projects span the full spectrum of application security, and a few themes stand out:

**Agentic AI Security** is a major focus, with multiple contributors — ashike25, Muhammad Daniyal, Nitin Awari, Regis, and stealthwhiz — building guardrail and detection frameworks for OWASP FinBot, one of the most forward-looking projects in the OWASP portfolio.

**Developer Tooling & Automation** shines through projects like Mradul Tiwari's work automating print-ready PDF generation for Cornucopia, sawankshrma's Angular 13→21 upgrade for DSOMM, and Md Kaif Ansari's ambitious BLT-Next migration from a Django monolith to a modern edge-based architecture.

**Security Knowledge & Intelligence** takes center stage with Manshu Saini and ParthAggarwal both working on OpenCRE, alongside PRATEEKSINGHWY's work on The Librarian module for the OWASP Agent.

**Community & Transparency** are at the heart of contributions from Anurag Yadav (OWASP Pulse & Contributor Recognition Program), Rudransh Shrivastava (Board Activity and Candidate Verification), and Harshit Verma (OWASP Community Snapshots).
