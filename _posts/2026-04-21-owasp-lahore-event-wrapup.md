OWASP LAHORE's most recent chapter event went smoothly and drew more than 300 participants - our largest turnout to date. From students to industry veterans, attendees filled every seat for an evening of talks, demos, and networking focused on modern application security.

<!--more-->

### Event at a glance

- **300+ attendees** across students, professionals, and security researchers
- Talks covering OWASP Top 10, secure coding, DevSecOps, and threat modeling
- Live demos of OWASP flagship projects (ZAP, Juice Shop, ASVS)
- Networking and Q&A with local and regional security leaders

### Thank you

A huge thank you to everyone who attended, spoke, volunteered, and helped us organize. The energy, questions, and conversations throughout the evening made this event a milestone for the Lahore security community.
